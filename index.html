OpenMower Map Editor

Real-world projection

Use the coordinates from your OpenMower config. You can read them with openmower config ros; use datum_lat and datum_long.

Hover for a tooltip.

Click a point to select it. Drag the selected point marker to move it. In add mode, click the map to insert a point after selection. In snap mode, click two existing points; all points between them snap to a straight line and are equally spaced.

Load a map to begin.
